## Pan Gravy
(2tbsp fat, 2tbsp flour, 1c liquid = 1c gravy)

### Ingredients

- (3c) Stock (left over from boiling a seitan roast)
- yellow onion, chopped
- mushrooms, sliced
- roux (5tbsp olive oil & 5tbsp flour)

### Instructions

- Caramelize onions
- Saut√© mushrooms
- create a roux
- pour in stock, wait for it to thicken
